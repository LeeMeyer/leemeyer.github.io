Privacy Policy for Waddle

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Waddle is designed to respect your privacy. The app does not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Information Waddle Collects

Waddle does not gather personal data, analytics data, account information, contact details, location data, browsing history, or any other information that can be used to identify you.

Local Storage Only

Waddle may use local storage on your device to save app preferences, settings, or other app state needed for the app to function properly. This information:

  • stays on your device;
  • is not sent to the developer or any third party;
  • is removed if you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Waddle does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote tracking tools within the app.

Children's Privacy

Because Waddle does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app's functionality or legal requirements. Any updated version will be published with a revised effective date.
